# TaxWise AI — Demo Video Script

A complete walkthrough script for creating a 5-10 minute demo video of the TaxWise AI platform.

## Equipment & Tools

- **Recording Software:** OBS Studio (free), ScreenFlow (Mac), or built-in screen recorder
- **Video Editor:** DaVinci Resolve (free), iMovie, or Adobe Premiere
- **Mic:** Decent microphone for clear narration (built-in is okay)
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920×1080) minimum
- **Frame Rate:** 30 FPS

---

## Recording Checklist

- ✅ Microphone works and audio is clear
- ✅ Screen resolution is 1080p
- ✅ Browser is zoomed appropriately (100% is often too small for viewers)
- ✅ Show keyboard shortcuts briefly (e.g., "Click here to..." or "Press Enter to submit")
- ✅ Narration is paced—not too fast, not too slow
- ✅ Background is distraction-free (close unnecessary tabs/windows)
- ✅ Browser cache cleared so pages load fresh (no stale data)
- ✅ Demo credentials (admin/admin123) are ready
- ✅ Internet connection is stable
- ✅ Backend is running on localhost:8000
- ✅ Frontend is running on localhost:5173

---

## Post-Production Tips

1. **Trim silence:** Edit out long pauses or loading times
2. **Add captions:** Auto-captions help accessibility; consider manual editing for accuracy
3. **Music:** Use royalty-free background music (keep it subtle)
4. **Transitions:** Fade between scenes rather than hard cuts
5. **Zoom:** Use subtle zoom effects on UI elements you're discussing
6. **Text overlays:** Add section titles (e.g., "Products Page", "AI Assistant")
7. **Cursor highlights:** Use screen recording software to highlight mouse movements
8. **Export:** Export as MP4 at 1080p/30fps for YouTube/social media
